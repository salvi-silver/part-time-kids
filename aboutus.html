About us

Party Time Kids specializes in creating unforgettable experiences for children through vibrant and engaging parties and activities in the Los Angeles area, including Glendale and Burbank. Our passionate team brings joy to every celebration, from birthday parties to school events. We also visit kindergartens, designing unique and fun programs tailored for K-12 students. With a variety of entertainment options—games, crafts, and interactive performances—we ensure every event sparks imagination and laughter. Let us make your child's special day a magical adventure they'll cherish forever!
